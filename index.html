Gwil made the rules, Gwil's handing out the points, Gwil's watching to see if you beat him.

Gwil-I-Am in charge of the points! Click for Gwil's rules:

Pick a score for every fixture, then rank how confident you are from 1 (not very) to 24 (lock it in). Call the right result and Gwil hands over your stake in points. Nail the exact score and Gwil doubles it, because he's generous like that. Get it wrong and he takes your stake back off you - because Gwil's not a charity and really wants to win. Top the matchweek and he throws in a lofty +5 bonus points - but only once every game that week has been played; Gwil's not paying out on a hunch. While the week's still live, whoever's out in front (likely Gwil because he's suspiciously good), gets a green outline, but nobody gets paid until it's official. If it's a tie for the bonus, Gwil meticulously checks who nailed the most exact scores, then who'd have won without the doubling - and if you're still tied after all that, fine, you both get paid. Is that right Gwil!? Good luck everyone!

To use: Type the actual live score in the fixture to adjust the leaderboard in real time, or click refresh fixtures button and everything else sorts itself out.
